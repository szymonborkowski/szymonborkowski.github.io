Beef Stew

Ingredients

Required:

  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Garlic clove
  • 1 Yellow pepper
  • 300g beef (stir-fry strips)
  • 400g can chopped tomatoes
  • Rosemary
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Optional:

  • 500ml beef stock
  • Carrots
  • Potatoes
  • Oregano
  • Basil
  • Green beans
  • Bread

Instructions

  1. Cook onion and garlic in olive oil for 2mins.
  2. Tip in the beef strips, fry for a few mins to brown the meat.
  3. Throw in the pepper, tomatoes, rosemary, beef stock, carrots, potatoes , oregano, basil, and green beans.
  4. Bring to a boil and simmer for 45mins.
