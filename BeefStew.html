Beef Stew

Ingredients

Required:

  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Garlic clove
  • 1 Yellow pepper
  • 300g beef (stir-fry strips)
  • 400g can chopped tomatoes
  • 500ml beef stock
  • Rosemary
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Optional:

  • Carrots
  • Potatoes
  • Oregano
  • Basil
  • Green beans
  • Bread

Instructions

  1. Cook onion and garlic in olive oil for 2 mins.
  2. Tip in the beef strips, fry for a few mins to brown the meat.
  3. Throw in the pepper, tomatoes, rosemary, beef stock, carrots, oregano, basil, and green beans.
  4. Add in the potatoes after 15 mins
  5. Bring to a boil and simmer for 1 hour.
