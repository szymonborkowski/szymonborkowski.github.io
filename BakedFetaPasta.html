Baked Feta Pasta

Ingredients

Required:

  • 500g-600g cherry tomatoes
  • 230g block of feta
  • 300g pasta
  • 3-5 cloves garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Crushed red pepper flakes

Optional:

  • 1 shallot (quartered)
  • 3 sprigs thyme
  • basil

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to around 200Â°C
  2. In an oven casserole dish combine tomatoes, shallot, garlic, and olive oil. Season with salt and red pepper flakes. Toss to combine.
  3. Place the feta block in the middle of the dish along with olive oil on top. Bake for 40-45mins until feta is gold on top and tomatoes are bursting
  4. Cook pasta in pot.
  5. Take the casserole dish out and squish the tomates and feta together into a sauce. You can add some of the pasta water.
