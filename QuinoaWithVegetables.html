Quinoa with vegetables

Ingredients

Required:

  • quinoa
  • broccoli
  • basil pesto
  • butter or olive oil
  • salt
  • pepper

Optional:

  • tomatoes
  • onions
  • peppers
  • mushrooms
  • spinach
  • shallots
  • garlic
  • shrimp
  • chicken

Instructions

  1. Boil quinoa and broccoli in a pot.
  2. In the meantime cook any vegetables you have chosen in a pan. Get the vegetables hot enough so that the water steams out. You don't want them to burn so add butter or olive oil.
  3. Add lots of salt and pepper to the vegetables.
  4. The quinoa should absorb all the water once it's done.
  5. Mix the quinoa and vegetables.
  6. Add around half a jar of basil pesto.
