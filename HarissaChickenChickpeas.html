Za'atr chicken with crushed harissa chickpeas

Ingredients

Required:

  • 1 onion
  • 1 red pepper
  • 1 yellow pepper
  • 400-500g chicken
  • 1 tablespoon za'atr
  • 400g chickpeas
  • 2 tablespoon red harrisa paste
  • 150g spinach
  • rapespeed/olive oil

Optional:

  • chopped parsley

Instructions

  1. Chop and fry the onion and peppers until soft and golden
  2. Mix some oil and za'atr and coat the chicken
  3. Put the chicken on a baking tray and grill the chicken in the oven until golden and cooked through (6-8mins, toss halfway)
  4. Heat up the chickpeas in a pan and a small bit of water. Roughly mash together. Wilt the spinah in a microwave with some water.
  5. Mix the chickpeas with the spinach, onions, peppers, and parsley.
