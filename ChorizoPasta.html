Recipe title

Ingredients

Required:

  • Red bell pepper
  • Chorizo sausage
  • Can of tomato sauce
  • Oil, salt, pepper, oregano, basil

Optional:

  • Parmesan
  • Spinach
  • Garlic

Instructions

  1. Chop up the bell peppers and cut up the chorizo.
  2. Heat up oil, fry the peppers, after a couple of mins throw in the chorizo.
  3. Add the can of tomatoes.
  4. Add salt, pepper, oregano, and basil.
  5. Add in the spinach and cook until wilted.
  6. Add pasta to dish and serve.
